## Ingredients
- Graham cracker crust
- 1 / 2 cup chocolate chips
- 3 ripe bananas
- 2 tsp brown sugar
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- Whipped cream

## Directions
1. Preheat oven to 375
2. Mash the bananas and combine with sugar, cinnamon, and chocolate chips
3. Cook in oven for 10 minutes
4. Cover in whipped cream
