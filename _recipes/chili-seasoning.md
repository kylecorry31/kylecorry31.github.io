## Ingredients

- 9 parts chili powder
- 3 parts cumin
- 3 parts salt
- 2 parts cilantro
- 1 part onion powder
- 1 part garlic powder
- 1 part pepper
- 1 part cayenne pepper

## Directions

1. Add all spices into a mason jar
2. Close lid and shake well to incorporate
