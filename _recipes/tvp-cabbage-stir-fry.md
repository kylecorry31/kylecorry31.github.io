## Ingredients

- 1 head of chopped Napa cabbage
- 1 cup of TVP (or crumbled tofu)
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 2 tsp ginger
- 2 tsp mushroom powder
- 1 tsp garlic
- 1/2 tsp salt
- 1/8 tsp pepper

## Directions

1. Rehydrate TVP
2. Saute cabbage over medium heat until wilted
3. Stir in TVP and spices
4. Cook until liquid evaporates
