## Ingredients

- [Mashed potatoes](/recipes/mashed-potatoes)
- Olive oil (substitute with other oil / butter)
- 1 bunch of kale 


## Directions

1. Chop and steam kale until dark green and pliable
2. Saute kale in olive oil
3. Stir kale into mashed potatoes
