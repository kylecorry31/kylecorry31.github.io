category: side
---

## Ingredients

- 2 - 3 potatoes (any kind)
- Salt
- Pepper
- Olive oil

## Directions

1. Slice potatoes to the desired shape
2. Lightly coat with olive oil
3. Dust with salt and pepper
4. Fry in air fryer at 375 for 15 minutes (optionally flipping halfway through)
