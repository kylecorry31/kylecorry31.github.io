## Ingredients
- 1 lb dried beans
- 1 tbsp salt
- 1/2 tbsp garlic powder
- 1 tsp chili powder
- Water

## Directions (Instant Pot)
1. Add beans and spices to instant pot
2. Add water until beans are covered by 1 inch
3. Cook on high pressure for 35 minutes
4. Natural release for 10 minutes
