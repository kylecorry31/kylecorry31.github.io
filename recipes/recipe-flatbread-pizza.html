Flatbread Pizza Recipe

7-27-2018

Looking for a simple meal to make for lunch or dinner? Just make a pizza with a flatbread, its super easy.

Ingredients

  • Flatbread
  • Spinach (and/or other vegetables)
  • Shredded cheddar cheese
  • Olive/canola oil

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Place a flatbread on a greased pan and coat with oil.
  3. Place spinach (and/or other vegetables) on the pizza and cover in shredded cheese.
  4. Bake for 15 minutes.
