Seasoned Broccoli Recipe

7-28-2018

A great and easy broccoli recipe.

Ingredients

  • Broccoli (steamed)
  • Canola / olive oil
  • Garlic powder, salt, and pepper (to taste)

Directions

  1. Pour layer of oil into a skillet
  2. Put stove on high
  3. Add steamed broccoli
  4. Add garlic powder, salt, and pepper to taste
  5. Cook for about 5-10 minutes (until broccoli starts to change color and becomes soft) and reduce heat
