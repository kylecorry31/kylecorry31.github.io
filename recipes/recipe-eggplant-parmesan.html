Italian Eggplant Parmesan Recipe

7-27-2018

Eggplant Parmesan makes a great and healthy dinner.

Ingredients

  • Eggplants
  • Eggs
  • Breadcrumbs
  • Marinara sauce
  • Provolone cheese

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
  2. Slice eggplant either vertically or horizontally.
  3. Pour breadcrumbs onto a plate.
  4. Crack 1 or more eggs into a bowl and beat them.
  5. Dip each eggplant slice into the eggs, then immediately into the breadcrumbs, coating both sides.
  6. Place breaded eggplant onto a greased tray.
  7. Bake for about 30 minutes, then add sauce and cheese and heat a bit more at 350 degrees until the cheese melts.
